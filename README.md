## About me.

I’m a doctoral researcher at KU Leuven in the Department of Earth and Environmental Sciences, within the Division Forest, Nature and Landscape Systems (RESECO & EARTHMAPPS). I work on environmental and sustainable planning, landscape ecology, remote sensing, and data‑driven approaches for sensing, monitoring, and modelling natural ecosystems.

With a background in Bioscience Engineering: Agro- and Ecosystems Engineering, my expertise includes machine learning, ecosystem modelling, and Earth observation. I’m currently part of the Deepv (2025–2026) project, where I develop methods to simulate ecosystem responses to climate extremes.
